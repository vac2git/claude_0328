Freshly Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

Freshly ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the Freshly iOS application.

1. Information We Collect

Freshly does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. All data you enter — including item names, categories, and expiry dates — is stored only on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.

2. Data Storage

  • All data is stored locally on your iPhone or iPad.
  • We do not use any external servers, databases, or cloud storage.
  • No data is transmitted over the internet.

3. In-App Purchases

Freshly offers an optional one-time in-app purchase ("Pro") to unlock additional features. All purchase transactions are handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or have access to your payment information.

4. Notifications

Freshly may send local notifications to remind you about upcoming expiry dates. These notifications are scheduled and delivered entirely on your device. No notification data is sent to any external server.

5. Third-Party Services

Freshly does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

6. Children's Privacy

Freshly does not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13. The app does not collect personal information from anyone.

7. Data Deletion

You can delete all app data at any time by deleting the Freshly app from your device. Uninstalling the app permanently removes all locally stored data.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
