Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add an item?
Tap the "Add Item" button on the Home tab or the "+" button on the Items tab. Enter the item name, select a category, and set the expiry date.
How do notifications work?
Freshly sends local notifications to remind you before items expire. You can configure reminders for 3 days before, 1 day before, and on the expiry day in Settings. All notifications are processed on your device — no internet required.
What does Freshly Pro include?
Freshly Pro is a one-time purchase ($2.99) that unlocks unlimited items (free version supports up to 15), custom categories with your own icons and colors, and waste statistics to help you reduce waste.
How do I restore my purchase?
Go to Settings and tap "Restore Purchases". This will restore your Pro upgrade if you've previously purchased it.
How do I change the language?
Go to Settings and select your preferred language. Freshly supports English, Korean, and Simplified Chinese. The change applies immediately without restarting the app.
Where is my data stored?
All your data is stored locally on your device. Freshly does not use any servers or cloud storage. Your data never leaves your device.
How do I delete my data?
You can delete individual items by swiping left on them in the Items tab. To delete all data, simply uninstall the Freshly app from your device.
